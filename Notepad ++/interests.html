In the recent few months it's been increasingly difficult to make time to go up and see the horses and exercise them with long college hours and long work hours too. Hopefully in the summer I'll be able to get back into horse-riding.

I love travelling to new places and trying to immerse myself in new cultures

U.A.E

The United Arab Emirates is probably my favourite place that I've visited. We went to Abu Dhabi first and visited many cultural places like The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.
This mosque is one of the largest mosques in the world. When we visited we had to wear an abaya in order to be respectful of the muslim people worshipping at the mosque.
At one time the mosque can accommodate a maximum of 41,000 people.


After visiting Abu Dhabi, we then travelled to Dubai to visit the Burj Khalifa, the Burj Al Arab and the Dubai Fountains.
The Burj Khalifa is located on the grounds of the Dubai Mall and is the tallest building in the world, standing at 828 metres high. It took 6 years and 12,000 workers in order to build the Burj Khalifa.

Fiji

Fiji is a country in the Pacific Islands, just north of New Zealand. I went to Fiji to visit my aunt, her husband, Hamish and two of my cousins, Kitty and Niamh that live there. Although the population is around 900,000 , 34% of the islands that make up Fiji are inhabited.
